FEMINIST COALITION

The feminist coalition is a group of young Nigerian feminists formed in July 2020
with a mission to champion equality for women in Nigerian society with a core focus
on education, financial freedom and representation in public office. We have a vision
for a Nigeria where equality for all people is a reality in our laws and everyday lives.
